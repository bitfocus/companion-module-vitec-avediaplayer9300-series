## Vitec AvediaPlayer 9300 series

Control module for the AvediaPlayer 9300 series IPTV Endpoints

The endpoint must have HTTPS Upgrade disabled under the services tab.

## Actions
- **Current Channel URI**
- **Mode**
- **Mute**
- **Teletext**
- **Volume**
- **Volume Up**
- **Volume Down**

## Variables
- **CurrentChannel URI**
- **Mode**
- **Volume**

## Feedbacks
- **Current Channel**
- **Mode**
- **Mute**
- **Teletext**
