## Vitec AvediaPlayer 93XX

Control module for the AvediaPlayer 93XX series IPTV Endpoints

The endpoint must have HTTPS Upgrade disabled under the services tab.

## Actions
- **Mode**
- **Set Channel URI**
- **Set Volume**
- **Volume Up**
- **Volume Down**

## Variables
- **Mode**
- **Channel URI**
- **Volume**

## Feedbacks
